Its okay to talk.

ANDYSMANCLUB is a mens mental health charity, offering free to attend peer-to-peer support groups for men across the UK. We support men with their mental health through conversation and positive action. #ITSOKAYTOTALK

Who we are

We break down the stigma of men's mental health.

ANDYSMANCLUB is a male-only support group where men aged 18 and over can come and get things off their chest and speak openly about their mental health in a judgement-free, non-clinical environment.

We have almost 130 groups across the UK, where each group meets at the same time each week, every Monday (except bank holidays) at 7pm.

With a service used on a weekly basis by over 3000 men and an army of over 900 volunteers on board, the movement is continuing to grow on a week by week basis. It takes true courage for a man to admit when hes down but #ITSOKAYTOTALK.

ANDYS MAN CLUB

THE FIRST RULE ABOUT ANDYSMANCLUB IS ITS OKAY TO TALK AT ANDYSMANCLUB.

Simply put, thats the whole point. Its a group where men dont have to battle to be heard or listened to, but where they can join the fight against reducing the rate of mental illness, depression and suicide in men.

Our Why

WE HAVE ONE AIM, TO HELP ONE GUY, TO SAVE ONE LIFE.

ANDYSMANCLUB takes its name from Andrew Roberts,

a man who sadly took his own life aged 23 in early 2016. Andy was a lovely and doting father, son, brother, grandson, nephew, cousin and friend, and was just 23 years old when he was taken away by suicide, sadly and tragically without warning.

On average one man every two hours takes his life in the UK. Its often said to be a factor is that men dont talk. Thats why ANDYSMANCLUB was born. Established in 2016 by Luke Ambler, Andys brother-in-law, the aim of the club is to break down these stigmas, support men struggling with mental health issues and lend a hand to suicide prevention.

That change starts with you...
